This page is completely based around where I believe I'll be in the future. Whether it's in six months or a few years, this is my rough outline for what I think will happen.

6 months

Hopefully in six months I will be in the middle of the fall semester of my senior year. With any luck, I will be able to have kicked a lot of my mental health issues in the ass and come out stronger than where I'm at currently. I will have completed my internshop at Case Western Reserve for the second year in a row, and I will begin looking for possible employment opportunities. I will also have my entire living situation figured out by that point and will have hopefully signed a lease for a new apartment closer to campus with a friend.

1 year

In a year, I'm hoping to be preparing for my own graduation. With any luck, I will have a job opportunity lined up, presumably either here at Kent or at Case Western Reserve.

5 years

In five years, I should be in a stable job, and, with any luck, I'll be looking to purchase a house for myself instead of renting. I would like to believe my best friend that just turned 4 will still be with me in 5 years, and she'll continue to be my partner in crime.

10 years

The plan is to have started a family in 10 years, or by the time I'm 31. If my dog has passed on by this point, I'll definitely have another friend by my side who will undoubtedly cause trouble everywhere she goes. Hopefully I will have my career completely in line and be very happy with where I'm at. I'm planning on staying somewhere in Northeast Ohio, but who knows where life will take me.
